X4

Intro#

OpenPPG X4 Controller uses WebUSB to communicate from your browser to your device.

Make sure you are using one of the supported browsers We recomend Chrome.

Get started#

  1. Plug the microUSB cable into your controller and connect to your PC or phone.
  2. You may see a notification pop up from your browser. Now navigate to the config tool.
